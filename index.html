Core rule

Users can like many, but hold only one live match.

Incoming likes remain visible, but while a user is matched they cannot continue swiping or open new conversations.

Intent separated

Casual and long-term pools never overlap.

Discovery candidates are strictly filtered by chosen intent so the product feels opinionated and clear.

Signal quality

Every right swipe includes a 1 to 10 interest score.

Mutual matches can immediately see how strongly the other person was interested before investing energy.

How the system works

A relationship path people can understand in seconds.

Instead of hiding the rules behind features, ATTRACT Shun makes the process visible: match, introduce, plan, decide. The structure itself becomes part of the product story.
